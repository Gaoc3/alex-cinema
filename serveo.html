How it works

Serveo is a dedicated remote port forwarding server. We provide a public bridge that routes traffic to your local environment using the protocols you already use.

Step 01

Connect

Connect via SSH, WireGuard, or our extension. No proprietary binary needed.

Step 02

Get your URL

Serveo instantly provides a public, encrypted URL that routes directly to your localhost.

Step 03

Share securely

Your tunnel is encrypted end-to-end. Share the URL for instant testing and demos.
